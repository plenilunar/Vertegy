About Vertegy

Vertegy is a full service sustainability consultant. Our structure allows us to work with building owners, designers, constructors and building occupants focusing solely on issues of sustainability. Evolving from a 20-year career in the construction industry, energized by the success of achieving LEED Platinum and four Green Globes certification on Alberici's Corporate Headquarters building, Vertegy has discovered the ability to apply economical suggestions to heighten the level of sustainability in any type of project.

Our philosophy towards sustainable buildings is different from that of most consultants. Vertegy utilizes an integrated process, which ultimately starts before the design phase of a project and is not complete until long after the tenant has moved into the building. This integrated process increases value for the building owner by:

  • Maximizing team synergy,
  • Applying correct sustainable strategies,
  • Reducing ongoing operational and maintenance expense and
  • Improving the quality of the built environment.

Our focus starts with the integration process and continues through the elements which define a sustainable building. When applied correctly, decisions regarding site issues, water use, resources and materials, energy consumption and conservation, quality of life, and emissions create innovative business solutions. Vertegy's experience working with numerous project teams has afforded us the opportunity to apply strategies to achieve a high level of sustainability in all categories.
