Sustainable Design, Development and Construction Consulting

Vertegy is a full service sustainability consultant. Whether the motivation of your team is to create a high performance building, create a low impact development, demonstrate an environmentally sensitive commitment or have a project certified by a third party such as LEED or Green Globes, Vertegy can help.

Our structure allows us to work with building owners, designers, constructors and building occupants, to integrate with their team and to assist in creating greater owner value and occupant satisfaction through the application of thorough and customized strategies focusing solely on issues of sustainability.
